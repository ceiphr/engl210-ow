Trials and Imprisonment of Oscar Wilde

ENGL-210 Project

Lord Alfred Douglas @bosie
Known to many as Bosie. I am the young lover of Wilde, after meeting him in the summer of 1891. Famously known in the scandal of Wilde as he was accused of being a sodomite, I encouraged him to sue for liable. Unfortunately, that led to the demise of my lover and I would therefore not see him until years later in France. WE still kept in contact through the letter “De Profundis” when he was imprisoned.

Birthday October 22, 1870
Education Winchester College, Magdalen College
Hometown Worchester, England
